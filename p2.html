Frequently Asked Questions about ShingYupsal

What is ShingYupsal?

ShingYupsal is our unique take on the traditional Korean BBQ experience, specializing in samgyupsal - grilled pork belly. We offer a variety of marinades and side dishes to complement the rich flavors of the meat, providing an unforgettable dining experience.

How do you eat ShingYupsal?

Guests at ShingYupsal enjoy their samgyupsal by grilling the meat right at their table, then wrapping it in lettuce leaves with garlic, green peppers, and a dab of ssamjang (a thick, spicy paste) or our house special sauces. Itâ€™s a fun, interactive way to enjoy your meal!
